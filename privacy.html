1. Overview

Gary Botman Personal Assistant is a private, single-user automated messaging service. The sole recipient of all messages is the account owner who built and operates this service. This service is not available to the general public.

2. How Consent Is Collected

The account owner provides explicit consent by configuring this system to send notifications to their own mobile phone number. By setting up and activating the service, the account owner consents to receive automated SMS messages including reminders and market notifications. This privacy policy, including all SMS disclosures, is reviewed and accepted by the account owner at the time of configuration.

3. Information Collected

The only personal information used by this service is:

  • The account owner's mobile phone number, used solely for SMS delivery
  • Message content required to deliver reminders and notifications

4. How Information Is Used

Information is used exclusively to deliver automated personal reminders and notifications (such as water intake reminders, market intelligence alerts, and task notifications) to the account owner. No information is used for any other purpose.

5. Data Sharing

Mobile phone numbers and personal information collected by this service are never shared with, sold to, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for marketing, promotional, or any other purposes. This is a private, single-user service with no third-party data sharing of any kind.

6. Message Frequency

Up to 10 messages per day may be sent depending on configured reminders and alerts. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

7. How to Opt Out (STOP)

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a one-time confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

8. How to Get Help (HELP)

Reply HELP to any message, or email aatmonamin@gmail.com for support.

9. Data Retention

The recipient's mobile number is stored locally on the account owner's private server and is not transmitted to any external database, analytics platform, or third party beyond the SMS carrier used for delivery.
